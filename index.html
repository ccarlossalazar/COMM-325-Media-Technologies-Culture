Course Description:

This course explores the historical, theoretical, aesthetic, and practical dimensions of media over the past century. It highlights how advancements in media technology have influenced social, cultural, and political spheres globally. By the end of the course, students will gain a solid understanding of key media concepts and theories, both traditional and contemporary, along with essential technical and creative skills for effective communication across various media platforms. As we navigate a networked, global, and interactive 21st century, students will develop the literacy needed to engage with the evolving languages of the digital age.
